Frequently asked questions
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, and we won't charge you again.
What's the difference between PDF and DOCX?
PDF files are print-ready and look exactly as designed — perfect for printing and distributing. DOCX files (Premium only) are editable in Microsoft Word or Google Docs, so you can customize vocabulary, add your school name, or adapt exercises for your class.
Do free worksheets require an account?
No — you can download up to 5 free worksheets per day without creating an account. Creating a free account removes that limit and lets you save favorites and track your downloads.
Can I use Workshido worksheets in my classroom commercially?
Yes. All worksheets on Workshido are licensed for classroom use, including paid language schools and academies. You may not resell or redistribute them outside of your own teaching context.
How does the 7-day free trial work?
Sign up for Premium and get full access for 7 days at no charge. You'll only be billed after the trial ends. Cancel before day 7 and you won't pay anything.
